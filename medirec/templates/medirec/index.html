About MadiRec.

MadiRec is a secure web platform designed to help doctors manage patient records. This system medical professionals to create, view, update, and delete patient files, record diagnoses, track treatment plans, and organize medical notes, all in one place. Our emphasise on simplicity, privacy, and functionality, ensures that patient information is well structured and accessible only to the assigned doctor. With features medical history tracking, and patient search, the application streamlines the record keeping process for healthcare providers.
